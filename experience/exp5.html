Platoon Leader, First Lieutenant

Mar 2022 - Jun 2024 South Korea

Overview

In the Republic of Korea Army, I served as a platoon leader responsible for team management, personnel development, and operational coordination. This experience shaped my leadership style and strengthened my ability to guide teams through structure, communication, and accountability in high responsibility environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • Led a 30 member platoon and managed personnel through structured communication and day to day operational oversight.
  • Conducted weekly counseling sessions to support team development, morale, and performance.
  • Built strong team cohesion through leadership, mentorship, and clear expectations.
  • Coordinated responsibilities across the platoon to maintain discipline, collaboration, and execution quality.

Achievements

  • Helped build one of the highest performing and most cohesive teams in the battalion through consistent leadership and mentorship.
  • Strengthened my skills in communication, people management, and decision making under responsibility.
  • Developed a leadership foundation that continues to influence how I work in research, engineering, and team based environments.
